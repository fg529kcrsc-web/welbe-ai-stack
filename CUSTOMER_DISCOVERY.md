# Customer Discovery

## Purpose

Track learnings from customer conversations, validate assumptions, guide product decisions.

---

## Key Questions

1. Who has the pain we're solving?
2. How acute is the pain (1-10)?
3. What are they doing today to solve it?
4. What would make them switch?
5. What would they pay?
