# Skill: Customer Discovery Synthesis

Extract signal from customer calls. Convert raw conversation into structured insight.

## When to Use

- After a discovery call with a roofing contractor or prospect
- After a demo, sales call, or follow-up conversation
- When reviewing call recordings or transcripts
- Weekly: synthesize across multiple calls to find patterns

---

## What to Listen For

### Pain Points
- Repetitive manual work ("I spend hours on X every week")
- Lost leads ("We miss calls when we're on a job")
- Cash flow problems ("Waiting on invoices kills us")
- Scheduling chaos ("Double-bookings, no-shows, last-minute cancellations")
- Follow-up failure ("We quote and never hear back")
- Estimating time ("Takes me 2 hours to write one estimate")
- Crew coordination ("Hard to know who's where, what's done")

### Objections
- Price ("Too expensive for a small shop")
- Trust ("Had bad experience with software before")
- Time ("No time to learn new tools")
- Tech aversion ("I'm not a tech person")
- Existing tools ("Already use ServiceTitan / Jobber / just Excel")
- Skepticism ("AI won't understand roofing")

### Willingness to Pay (WTP) Signals
- Direct: "What does it cost?" (asked early = high interest)
- Indirect: "How do other shops pay for this?"
- Budget anchors: mentions of what they currently spend on tools/staff
- ROI framing: "If it saves me X hours..." or "Even one extra job a month..."
- Resistance: "I'd never pay more than $X/month for software"

### Buying Process
- Who decides? (owner alone, partner, office manager)
- Current tools and contracts (switching cost)
- Timeline ("We're slammed until fall")
- Trigger event ("We just lost a big contract", "Hired someone new")

## Documentation Template

Fill this out within 30 minutes of the call while memory is fresh.

```markdown
## Call: [Name] — [Company] — [Date]

**Contact**: [Name], [Role]
**Company size**: [# crew, # jobs/month estimate]
**Location**: [City, State]

### Top Pain (in their words)
> "[Direct quote from them describing biggest problem]"

### Pain Points Identified
- [Pain 1]
- [Pain 2]
- [Pain 3]

### Objections Raised
- [Objection 1] → [how they framed it]
- [Objection 2] → [how they framed it]

### WTP Signal
- Estimated budget: $[X]/month
- Signal type: [direct ask / anchor / ROI framing / resistance]
- Quote: "[relevant quote if any]"

### Buying Process
- Decision maker: [name/role]
- Current tools: [list]
- Timeline: [immediate / Q3 / "someday"]
- Trigger: [what's driving them to look now]

### Next Step
- [ ] [Specific action with date]

### Fit Score
- Pain match (1–5): 
- WTP match (1–5): 
- Timeline match (1–5): 
- Overall: [Hot / Warm / Cold]
```

---

## Synthesis: Patterns Across Calls

Run this weekly or after every 5 calls.

### Questions to answer
1. What pain came up in 3+ calls? → That's a core feature.
2. What objection came up most? → That's a messaging problem.
3. What's the most common WTP range? → That's your price anchor.
4. Who said yes fastest? → That's your ICP.
5. What surprised you? → That's a hypothesis to test.

### Output format
```markdown
## Weekly Synthesis — [Week of DATE]

**Calls this week**: [N]

**Top 3 pains** (by frequency):
1. [Pain] — mentioned in X/N calls
2. [Pain] — mentioned in X/N calls
3. [Pain] — mentioned in X/N calls

**Top objection**: [Objection] — [how to address it]

**WTP range**: $[X]–$[Y]/month (median: $[Z])

**ICP signal**: [Description of the customer who moved fastest/showed most interest]

**Hypothesis to test**: [One thing to validate next week]
```

---

## Success Criteria

A synthesis is complete when:

- [ ] Call notes filled within 30 min of conversation
- [ ] At least one direct quote captured verbatim
- [ ] Pain points ranked by severity (not just listed)
- [ ] WTP signal recorded even if indirect
- [ ] Fit score assigned (Hot / Warm / Cold)
- [ ] One specific next step with date
- [ ] Weekly pattern synthesis done after every 5 calls
