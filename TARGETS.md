## Outreach Templates

### Cold call opener (under 30 seconds)
> "Hey, is this [Owner name]? My name is Stan, I help roofing contractors in [state] automate their lead follow-up and estimating using AI. Quick question — when you're on a job, how do you handle inbound calls? ... Are you losing any of those?"

### Email subject lines
- "Lost leads when you're on a roof?"
- "2 hours to write an estimate?"
- "Your competitor is responding to leads in 5 minutes"

### Qualification questions
1. How many inbound leads do you get per week?
2. How long does it take you to respond to a new lead?
3. How do you write estimates right now?
4. What happens to leads you don't get back to within 24 hours?
5. What tools are you currently using? (CRM, scheduling, estimate software)

## Research Notes

- 79.1% of US roofing contractors are single-owner operations (100K+ shops)
- Top states by volume: TX (17K), FL (11K), CA (10K)
- Pain is consistent: manual quoting, missed calls, no follow-up system
- WTP anchor: most small shops spend $0–$200/month on software; ROI framing needed
- Best trigger events: recent storm damage in area, just lost a big contract, new hire
